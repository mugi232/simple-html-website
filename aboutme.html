A Passionate Teacher
Hello Friends. Once again, thank you for visiting my website and taking your time to get to know me better. My name is Gobi Krishnan and I am 30 years old. I am working as a full time primary school teacher at Teluk Intan, Perak. I educate children between the ages of 7 to 12 under prescribed curriculum to develop literacy and numeracy skills of the students.

Additionally, I also use various effective techniques to engage students with the learning process according to their age and individual abilities.

Currently, my subject areas are Mathematics, English, Science and Tamil.

My Working Hours
In addition to working regular school hours, sometimes I am also required to work extended hours in order to plan lessons, attend staff meetings or school events, parents meeting and mark students'school homeworks.

I usually carry out these tasks after teaching hours or weekends.

Daily tasks for a Primary School Teacher
  • Preparing daily and long-term lesson plans according to curriculum guidelines.
  • Teaching a full range of subject areas.
  • Maintaining productive working habits and discipline in the classroom.
  • Developing children’s interests, abilities and coordination using a variety of creative activities.
  • Attending staff meetings and training and development sessions.
  • Assessing and evaluating students’ educational progress and abilities.
